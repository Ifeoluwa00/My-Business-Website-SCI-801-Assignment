Frequently Asked Questions

What are the entry requirements for your programs?

+

Entry requirements vary by program. For foundational programs, basic IT knowledge is required. For advanced certifications, prior experience or relevant education is recommended. Contact our admissions team for specific requirements.

Do you offer payment plans?

+

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans for all our programs. You can pay in 2-4 installments depending on the program duration and cost. Corporate billing options are also available for organizational training.

+
