About Ifeoluwa Cyber Institute

Building Africa's cybersecurity capacity through excellence in governance, risk, and compliance education.

Our Story

Ifeoluwa Cyber Institute (ICI) is a professional training and capacity development institution dedicated to building Africa's next generation of Information Security Professionals. Founded with a vision to close the cybersecurity skill gap across Africa, ICI empowers individuals and organizations to protect digital assets, manage risk intelligently, and achieve full regulatory compliance in today's evolving cyber landscape.

We deliver globally relevant, locally contextualized training designed to equip learners with theoretical knowledge, practical expertise, and professional ethics to thrive in both corporate and public sector roles. At ICI, we believe that cybersecurity begins with strong governance, accountability, and leadership—not just technology. Every course, lab, and project is crafted to develop professionals who can make informed security, compliance, and governance decisions.

Our Vision

To be Africa's most trusted and transformative center for Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC) and Information Security education, building competent professionals who uphold integrity, drive resilience, and lead organizational security transformation.

Our Mission

  • Deliver world-class GRC and cybersecurity education aligned with global frameworks and best practices.
  • Equip professionals with skills to identify, assess, and mitigate risks while ensuring compliance with regulatory standards.
  • Promote a culture of integrity, governance, and accountability within Africa's digital ecosystem.
  • Foster research, innovation, and collaboration in information security governance.
  • Build strategic partnerships with global institutions to strengthen cybersecurity and compliance capacity across the continent.
